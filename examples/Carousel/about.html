About Us:

Yardsale is a trusted community marketplace for people to list and rent what would otherwise be unused spaces. From a driveway for local parking in a jiffy, to a front yard for an all day tailgate, Yardsale enables existing space to be optimally utilized by the community.

With world-class customer service and a growing community of users, Yardsale is the easiest way for people to monetize their extra space and showcase it to an audience of millions.

This is a unique service that matches people who need space and people who are not using their space. For example at the University of Virginia, people are forced to find parking off-campus during home football games. This leads to people’s cars getting towed and a lot of pain and hassle. This can be completely avoided with Yardsale. Many people have an open space in their driveway, so they could rent those spaces out to those who need it.
